About BISV Hacks 2025

What is BISV Hacks?

BISV Hacks 2025 marked our inaugural hackathon, bringing together over 70 passionate students from across the Bay Area. Held on March 1st, 2025, at BASIS Independent Silicon Valley, the event was a tremendous success that showcased the incredible talent and creativity of our participants.

Theme & Focus

The theme of our hackathon was to address issues of social justice and promote social good, which challenged participants to create innovative solutions addressing real-world problems. Throughout the day, students worked tirelessly on projects ranging from environmental sustainability to educational technology.

Experience & Learning

Participants had access to mentorship from industry professionals, engaging workshops, and networking opportunities. The event featured guest speakers from leading tech companies who shared their insights and experiences with the innovators.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon? +

A hackathon is an event in which a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative or competitive computer programming. In our case, collaborative programming.

Can I submit projects from other hackathons? +

As long as it fits the theme and prompt, yes.

Who can participate? Is coding experience needed? +

Any high school student with a passion for technology can participate, even if they have no coding experience!

Do I need a team? +

You can come solo or with up to 5 teammates.

Will the hackathon be judged in-person or online? +

The hackathon will be judged online and not in-person due to time constraints. The results will be sent out by 5:00 p.m. on March 15th.

Where will the hackathon be held? +

The hackathon will be held at BASIS Independent Silicon Valley. The school's adress is 1290 Parkmoor Ave, San Jose, CA 95126.

What do I need to bring? +

Just you and your laptop!

Is there going to be food? +

Yes, lunch will be held, and there will be snacks served throughout the day.
